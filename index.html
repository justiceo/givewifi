Give Techie Learners Free, Fast and Unlimited Internet

For just $20, you can ensure that a tech hub serving up to 70 learners has free, fast, and unlimited internet access for an entire month. Learn more about how it works.

How It Works

In Nigeria's tech hubs, learners bring bring their internet (mobile hotspot), which on average would cost them N10K-N30K in a month.

Give WiFi is a non-profit that replaces the student's slow, data-capped and expensive mobile hotspot with a fast, free and unlimited-data satellite internet.

A monthly subscription for satellite internet costs about N35K, which translates to $20. We work with tech hubs that have 50 or more learners to install and run a satellite internet service.
